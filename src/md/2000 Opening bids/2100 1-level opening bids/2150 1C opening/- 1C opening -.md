### <a name="1C_opening"> {1C} opening

All unbalanced hands with ca. 11-19 HCP and without a 4-card or longer major suit - and all balanced (4333, 4432, and 5332) hands with 18-19 HCP - are opened {1C}. This bid is forcing.
